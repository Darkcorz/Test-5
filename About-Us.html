Why Buy From Us?

  Our solar panels incorporate recycle parts from used solar panels. We are able to recycle 80% of the old solar panels into new ones, allowing us to support minimal effects on the environment. Not only retaining high quality and lower cost than most companys, SunRise has a lifetime warranty on any type of solar panel you purchase!

SunRise History

     Created in 1970, SunRise was originally a milk brand until 1980. The founder Jim Putin, who was a simple farmer, cultivated his small business delivering milk bottles until his death in 1980. His son Vladimir Putin took over the business and decided to change the course of the business. His desire for renewable energy leads him to develop one of the first solar panels in 1982. In 2006, the solar panel market started to increase drastically, which caused Henry Crow to develop a new solar panel made mostly from recycled solar panels. This desire for self-sustaining energy with a very minimal environmental impact is where SunRise shines the best!
